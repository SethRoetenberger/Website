Medieval Armor

I took this project on over winter break during my first year of college. After lot of research as to how armor worked, I decided to use 18-gauge steel and began creating the curves of the armor with hammering and gentle bending. It took me an entire month of cold and hot forming to create this suit of armor. The lower arm pieces were the most difficult pieces to make. This is because it includes multiple moving parts that all fold over each other so that all parts of the arm are covered during motion. The rest of the armor included similar techniques which cause the material to fully cover your body while still allowing for movement.

This project is one of my favorites because I learned that a material like steel, which is commonly thought of as hard, can be flexible under the right conditions.
