Tables

There have been three different tables that I have created over the years. I started off by making a resin world table. I created this table by gluing two oak boards together and removing the wood that would be the ocean with a router. I then poured in the resin and sanded down the wood until it was flush with the resin. This process took a long time and if I were to do it again, I would plane it down instead. I had never worked with resin before, and this helped me understand another material and how to use it.

The second table that I made was a large two-by-four table. This was made from several different 2x4's that I glued together until I got a strong board. I then used a router to make the surface flush. After that I coated the boards in resin until I got a smooth finish. I added some legs and now it is a strong and heavy-duty table.

The final table that I created was based off a previous resin table attempt. I glued several pine boards together and then coated them in multiple layers of resin to get an ocean wave look.
