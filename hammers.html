Hammers

I started fabricating these hammers by creating paper models and tracing them onto steel. I cut the parts out with an angle grinder and welded them together. These were my first experiences with welding, and it took me awhile to fully get the hang of it. It was not until I started beveling the edges that I really began to get good welds. This increase in my welding skill can be seen in the later axe hammer, which I used spot welding and miters to get better penetration. I added a patina to the axe hammer by applying vinegar and then neutralized it with baking soda.
